About Muratec

Built by an
engineer and founder.

Muratec is the consulting and product arm of entrepreneur and software engineer Ermin Muratović — Austrian-Bosnian, based in Lisbon. With over 9 years of hands-on experience across IT infrastructure, full-stack development, systems engineering, and business operations, Muratec delivers what most consultancies can't: execution alongside strategy.

The philosophy is simple: technology should serve the business, not complicate it. Every engagement is approached from first principles — understanding the operation before touching the stack.

Muratec operates at the intersection of Central European precision and Balkan entrepreneurial intensity — bringing enterprise thinking to growing businesses across Portugal, Austria, and the Western Balkans.
